STILL

This two-piece tool plays with body-to-mind communication, through personalized design. It consists of one object claiming space in my home, one object accompanying me through my day-to-day, and two photo series that explores its benefits.

2021

My experience with anxiety and is very physical, and I have grown to understand that this is because I refuse to confront fear. In this work, I chose to use this dynamic in me, to my advantage. By personifying the entity of trust, I give it space in the physical world and open the possibility of nurturing a healthy relationship with it. Instead of fright or flight, I choose to be still, and listen to my body.
