All those who have walked this Earth have left traces behind of their experiences, thoughts, relationships, and their very existence. Those who used the Alba were going through similar stages of life as current university students, and wanted to find their place in society. We bring the Alba Amicorumâ€™s hidden stories to the youth of today through our senses.

We brought the stories of people who kept an Album Amicorum alive through tactile, audio and visual traces. Through an explorative combination of physical and digital media, we invite you to connect and play.

In collaboration with the Koninklijke Bibliotheek of The Hague, we built an interactive 4-piece installation in response to their archive Alba Amicorum.

Exhibition showing at the Koningklijke Bibiotheek in the center of The Hague, during May and June of 2022.

How can we relate to experiences captured by these Alba Amicorum, and what could we learn about ourselved from them?

We were interested in the Alba Amicorum as a medium for people to leave behind traces of their existence. Those who kept an Album, usually began to do so around our age. We therefore chose to design this installation with the students of today in mind.

Focusing on the social interactions documented in these books, we bringing the stories of these people back to life in a contemporary context.
