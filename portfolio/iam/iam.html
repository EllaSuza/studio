I AM, AM I

A conversation between women on performative sexuality and femininity.

2021

JULIE GOSLINGA
NINE DE WIT
ANOUSCHKA VREESWIJK
BEELD EN GELUID DEN HAAG

As a part of Dutch Media Awards, we worked with the extensive archive of Beeld en Geluid. During these three days we weaved in our own media such as 3D models alongside footage that spoke to us from this dutch archive. Our conversations quickly stirred towards the long-lasting expectations and performative roles women take on when it comes to sexuality and sensuality.
