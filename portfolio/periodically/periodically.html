PERIODICALLY TRUE

Artistic research into a 29-day type living

27th of June - 02 of July 2024

Exhibited at the Graduation Show of Royal Academy of Arts, The Hague

I am not just a menstruating human when I bleed out of my vagina. The physical output of blood is a material developing continuously. The cycle that drives my body, 29 days at a time. teaches me that I am capable of different things at different times. What if, instead of flattening out these states of being, I let it radiate?

"Periodically True" invites you into my journey of collaborating with my menstrual cycle and its materials as a resource for introspective creativity. A synchronized body and calendar surrounded by hand-crafted blood paper and seasonal self-portraits builds upon the ever-growing recognition of the menstrual cycle as a powerful tool. This work was developed alongside the research of the Menstrualsphere.
